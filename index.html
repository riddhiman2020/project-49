Let's learn about some animals

PANDA

1. Pandas are one of the cutest animals found in the jungle

2. Their favourite food is bamboo

3. They are very good at camouflaging from predators

CAMEL

1. Camels are known as the ships of the desert

2. They survive for a long period of time without water

3. They store food in their humps,so when a camel had no food, that means his stored food is finished

CHAMELEON

1. Chameleons are known for their camouflaging skills

2. They can change their color according to their background to hide from predators

3. Their tougue are roughly twice their body's length
